Description

The dish you see above is a simply prepared meal of mashed potatoes. It is a very delicious one and worth eating.

Ingredients

  • Potatoes
  • Milk
  • Rama
  • Salt

Steps Taken to prepare the meal

    Boiled water inside a pot
    Put in the potatoes
    Pour in two teaspoons of salt
    Mash the potatoes when done
